My Best JavaScript Post!!

Posted on 27 March in 2024

Every programmer has a post which they think is their best. So do I. My best post in JavaScript is the "Story of Winston".

The title may seem very direct and boring but there are many surprises that await you in this story.

The first thing to remember is that at every slide something will happen once you click on the screen (appart from the "Next slide" button) Now on to the tour!
In the first slide if you click on the screen then our dear friend Winston appears saying hi to us!!
After clicking next the next slide we see baby Winston. So cute!! When we click on the screen a toy appears where our mouse was. Each time we click a different toy appears. Then Winston grows up! After clicking he say that he wants to be a rock star! His wish came true!! Winston entered his rock star age and played the drums like a true musician. Click a play the drums beat sound! In the last slide Winston has babies!! When you click the screen you are able to make more babies!!

I hope you liked reading it. Thanks for reading!!
