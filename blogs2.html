My 2nd Best JavaScript Post!!

Posted on 26 March in 2024

All programmers have a 2nd best game. So do I. My best game is the "Beaver go stick game".

In level 1 we control a beaver and we have to help it by collecting sticks for it without making the beaver fall into the holes in the ground. In level 2 we help the beaver by avoiding bugs that try to eat the sticks of the beaver. We also cannot bring the beaver to land otherwise the sticks are lost. I was very excited while making it.

I had first thought of making holes as the obtacle in level 2 but that didnt work out. So I made the holes on ground that cut points. In level 2 I wanted to make holes on the ground but I didn't like that idea because I would get boring and a bit too easy. So I cut points when the beaver hits the ground.
